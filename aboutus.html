About Us

You can’t buy happiness, but you can buy dessert and that’s kind of the same thing.

Le Crépuscule Dessert Shop was established in the year of 1971. We knew that we couldn’t bring french culture to the streets of Malaysia, but we wanted to make our desserts available to people whenever they’re up for a little treat. There’s just something about French desserts and the French’s culture of eating those desserts that appealed greatly to us. We believe everyone deserves to derive pleasure from simple things like eating your favorite dessert. Whether you're craving something sweet or savoury, you'll find your cup of tea here!

Our Products

At Le Crépuscule, we’ve always striving for perfection as every single product is baked without preservatives and artificials to guarantee freshness and healthiness. Our desserts are made by the finest ingredients and authentic baking techniques.
