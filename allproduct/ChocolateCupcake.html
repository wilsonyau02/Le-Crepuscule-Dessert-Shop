Chocolate Cupcake

We would like to claim our Death by Chocolate Cupcakes the best chocolate cupcake in town. It is moist with exceptionally smooth and rich Chocolate cream, topped with little Golden pearls sitting on them. Choose a box of 12 which can be served as a quick bite when you are having a mini gathering with your friends or served as a sweet treat in any occasion.

  • Keep refrigerated (0 - 4°C) for at least 30 minutes upon receiving. (DO NOT FREEZE)
  • Avoid direct sunlight or humid environment.
  • Best serving temperature: 10°C - 12°C; Take the cake out from the fridge shortly before serving
  • Best consumed within 2 days, however the cake can last in the chiller up to 4 days
